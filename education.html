About the Materials

All pieces are shoreline finds from the Niagara region, shaped naturally by water and time.

Shoreline Stones

Waves naturally smooth stones over decades. Each piece carries its own texture, pattern, and story.

Sea Glass & Quartz

Sea glass and quartz veins are rarer finds that require more time to prepare and curate.

Care

  • Wipe gently with a soft cloth
  • Avoid harsh chemicals
  • Store separately when possible

Pricing Philosophy

Pay what feels fair, within transparent minimums. This supports sustainable, local creation.
