How It Works

Pay What You Think Is Fair

Each listing has a suggested minimum to help cover materials and time. You’re invited to pay based on what the piece is worth to you. This keeps handmade jewelry more accessible while supporting local, small-batch work.

What’s Included

Each pendant listing includes a pendant and chain unless noted otherwise. Want a different chain length or a custom piece? Email me — I’m happy to make it personal.

Claim a Piece

Click any “Claim” button to email me. First come, first served — I’ll confirm availability, total, and pickup/shipping.
