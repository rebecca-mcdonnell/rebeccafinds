How It Works

Everything here is collected by hand, cleaned, photographed, and shared with care. Minimums keep this sustainable and cover real material costs plus the time it takes to curate one-of-one finds.

Pay what feels fair

You’re invited to contribute what feels fair to you. Minimums help cover chains + pendants, plus the time spent finding, cleaning, setting, photographing, and sharing each piece.

Jewelry pieces $20+ minimum • suggested $25+
Statement stones $30+ minimum • suggested $40+
