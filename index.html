About

A little shoreline magic, made real.

I’m Rebecca—based in St. Catharines and constantly outside, collecting and photographing the kind of small discoveries that make a day feel special.

Rebecca Finds is where my shoreline stones, sea glass, and quiet little treasures turn into wearable pieces—simple, intentional, and one-of-one.

(And yes—there are cats. Always.)

How it works

Easy, personal, and no-pressure.

  1. Pick a stone

    Choose from the available rocks gallery (RF codes) or ask for a color theme.

  2. Tell me your vision

    Gold/silver, pendant/keychain, minimal/detailed—anything helps.

  3. I confirm details

    I’ll confirm the plan + timeline, and we’ll keep it simple.

  4. Made & shared

    Your piece is finished, photographed, and ready for pickup/shipping.

Note: Stone availability changes quickly. If a stone is claimed, I’ll suggest the closest match.
