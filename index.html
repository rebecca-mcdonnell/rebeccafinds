How It Works

Simple, calm, and human.

1) See something you love

Browse the stones above and pick the one that keeps calling you back.

2) Claim / ask a question

Send a message. I’ll confirm availability and answer any questions.

3) Pay what you think is fair

You choose your price based on value + your budget. The minimum keeps materials and time covered.

4) Optional: turn it into jewelry

If it suits the stone, we can turn it into a one-of-one pendant.
