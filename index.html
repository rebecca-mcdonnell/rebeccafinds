How it works

Clear, respectful, and easy to follow.

1) Choose a piece

Browse the stones above and message the name of the piece you want to claim.

2) Pay what you think is fair

Each listing shows a minimum. If you can contribute more, it supports time, materials, and keeps future work accessible.

3) Pickup or delivery

Local Niagara meetups are preferred. Shipping can be arranged for a cost, when needed.

4) Custom options

Want a pendant or a more finished piece? Ask about custom settings and chains.
