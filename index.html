One-of-One Jewelry

Shoreline finds, carefully finished into wearable pendants. Chain available for every piece if requested.

Rebecca Finds is an independent jewelry project based in Niagara, Ontario. Each pendant begins as a locally collected shoreline find and is finished by hand in small batches with an emphasis on quality, longevity, and clear communication.

The work is intentionally low-waste and not mass-produced, with thoughtful pricing designed to keep it accessible.

Every listing is one-of-one. Once it is claimed, it is no longer available.
