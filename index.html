How it works

Simple, human, and fair.

1) Choose a stone

Pick one you love and tap Claim.

2) Tell me what feels fair

Minimum $15. After that, you choose based on the piece + your budget.

3) I make it wearable

We confirm the setting (gold/silver, chain length), then I create the final piece.

This model works because the community is honest. If you want a “suggested range,” ask — I’ll give one.
