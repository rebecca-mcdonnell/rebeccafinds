How it works

This is simple on purpose: choose what you love, pay what feels fair (within the minimum), and you’re supporting a local, low-waste, small-batch practice.

1) Pick your piece

Everything is one-of-one. If you see something you love, message me with the name of the stone/piece.

2) Choose your price

Pay what you think is fair—based on the minimum shown. Minimums keep materials and time covered, and help this stay sustainable and local.

3) Claim + arrange pickup/shipping

I’ll confirm availability, total, and pickup/shipping details. Local Niagara pickup is easiest.

Minimums (transparent + consistent)

  • $17 minimum for most stones/pieces in this drop
  • $20 minimum for Sea Glass, Quartz Vein, and Golden
  • Want to go above the minimum? It helps fund tools, chains, glue, and future finds
  • Want to stay right at the minimum? That’s welcome too

The goal is beautiful, local, sustainable pieces that don’t feel gatekept. If you’re unsure what’s fair, tell me your budget and we’ll make it work.
